Game Instructions
  • Each game can have 1-8 players.
  • Assign 1 person to be the "Score Keeper" who reads and follows the instructions below.
  • Each game consists of 4 turns or "Quarters".
  • Each player throws all four darts per Quarter.
  • To score points a dart must be TOUCHING a football with points AFTER all darts have been thrown.
  • Add up all points after your turn and that's your score for that Quarter.
Score Keeper Instructions
  • Click "New Game" button above.
  • Enter number of players. Must be 1-8.
  • Enter the display name for each player.
  • Enter the score for the current quarter for the current player.
  • The "Score Board" section shows each players score per quarter & their total score.
  • When the game is complete the winners name will be displayed. There is no support for ties at this point.
